About.

Edoardo Spadoni was born in Pesaro in 1992 and started to approach music when he was 10 years old.
In 2004 he began to study organ and composition at the Pesaro music conservatory.
When he was 17 he wrote his first piece and over the years enriches the repertoire with other unpublished pieces that he will decide to publish in his first album in 2018.
